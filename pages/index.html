Rutejìmo couldn’t wait until the day when the elders of the clan declared him an adult. However, the rites of passage were draped in secrecy, and he didn’t know when they would drag him out into the desert to test give him the chance to feel the wind of Shimusògo, the clan spirit, against his face. But, as the days crawled on, he wondered if they would ever accept him as a man. When it happened, he wasn’t expecting it. Instead of a dramatic display worthy of legend, he quickly found himself overwhelmed as he and four other teenagers are abandoned in the sands to either touch the clan spirit or die.

Blood and Sand is the first of three novels of Shimusogo Rutejìmo's life. It is an action, coming-of-age tale of someone who would never become a great fighter or powerful at magic. Instead, it is a gritty tale of wilderness survival, working together, and finding one’s place in the barbarous world of the desert clans.
