Journals of Fedran

D. Moonfire

Journals of Fedran is a collection of stories, essays, and poems set in the world of Fedran, a fantasy world by D. Moonfire.

Issue #01: Religion

This issue is only available for those who contribute via Patreon.

Religion is a thread that weaves throughout the tapestry of life. It doesn't matter if it is a shopkeeper who whispers prayers in hope of a quick sale or a priest who founded his own religion while struggling with his own faith. There are a wide variety of religions and faiths in Fedran and this issue tries to explore many of them.

Issue #00: Resonance

The premier issue of Journals of Fedran, this focuses a critical aspect of the world, resonance. Resonance affects how magic works, and more importantly, how it reacts poorly to other magic. This resulted in the invention of steam engines and gave birth to the beginning of the Industrial Age.
